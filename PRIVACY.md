# Privacy Policy for Word Me

**Last updated:** January 14, 2026

**Word Me** ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome Extension.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
We do not store, sell, or share your personal data. 

**Website Content (Text Selection):**
When you use the "Word Me" feature (by right-clicking a word), the specific text you selected is temporarily transmitted to third-party services solely for the purpose of fetching definitions and translations.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We use the following trusted APIs to provide functionality:
* **Free Dictionary API:** To provide definitions and phonetics.
* **MyMemory Translation API:** To provide language translations.

The text you select is sent directly to these services. We do not retain this data on any servers, and it is discarded immediately after the definition is displayed to you.

## 3. User History & Tracking
* We do **not** track your browsing history.
* We do **not** use cookies or trackers.
* We do **not** collect your IP address or location data.

## 4. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues page.
